Medical Help:

To help government make fact based policies so as to reach maximum number of people and combat maximum health concerns with effective planning backed by data-oriented approach. This would aim at achieving higher rate of prevention for the prevalent diseases and help in providing efficient treatment to improve health care.

Government can then use this data that is sent by the hospitals in various forms:
• Using the Report, The government can keep an eye on the rising diseases in an area on regularbasis and in turn can implement its policies more effectively at regular intervals.
• The Report would also help the government understand the needs of the hospitals (Govt. and Private) such as upgrading with proper machines & other infrastructure which would have positive impact on future patient’s health.
• The government can also use this data to decide on the number of seats required to fill in various departments for admission in the medical colleges and produce/train team of doctors, nurses’ etc. to address the problem of shortage of doctors and specialists based on requirement.
